Important: Create a Gmail Account Before Signing Up

Before you can sign up for IntelliSeason, you need to have a Gmail account using your Omantel ID. If you don’t have one, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Google Account sign-in page.
  2. Click on 'Create account' and select 'For myself.'
  3. Choose the option to 'Use your existing email.'
  4. Follow the prompts to complete the account creation process.

Sign in to IntelliSeason using your Gmail account:

  1. Go to the IntelliSeason sign-in page.
  2. Select the option to 'Signup.'
